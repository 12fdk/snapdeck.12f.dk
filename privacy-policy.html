SnapDeck AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

SnapDeck AI is built with privacy as a core principle. All AI processing happens entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or study materials.

Data Collection

SnapDeck AI does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No photos, PDFs, or text content is uploaded to any server
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are embedded in the app
  • No cookies or identifiers are stored

On-Device Processing

All AI-powered flashcard generation uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Your study materials (photos, PDFs, text) are processed locally on your iPhone and never leave the device.

Data Storage

Your flashcard decks and study progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not synced to any cloud service and remains entirely under your control.

In-App Purchases

SnapDeck AI offers optional premium subscriptions through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

SnapDeck AI does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

SnapDeck AI does not knowingly collect information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at 12f.dk.
